Established in 1999, Rio Estates has its offices in Riogordo, a typical Andalucian village, nestled in some of the most stunning countryside with spectacular views of the surrounding mountains. It is an area dedicated to olive and almond farming, also orange and lemon orchards and vineyards, a wonderful climate and location for enjoying walking and picnics. Malaga airport and the beaches are under 30 minutes drive from practically all of our Properties.

Since 2008 we have been working in Malaga Capital and have a large range of properies in the historic centre, Pedregalejo, El Palo, El Candado and Limonar.

RioEstates aim to find you the perfect Rural Country Home, Village House or Malaga Apartment and to make the whole experience a thoroughly enjoyable one backed by an experienced multi lingual company.
